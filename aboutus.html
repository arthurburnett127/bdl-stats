    • About us

    Blue Otter League was founded in December 2018 with the small goal of providing a fun and unbiased tournament. As our small league grew, so did our goals. Currently, our focus lies in the Gold and Platinum ranked brackets with aspirations to include Diamond+ tournaments in the near future. We strive to uphold the values of community, individual/team growth, sportsmanship, and competitiveness while promoting inclusion and respect among all members.
